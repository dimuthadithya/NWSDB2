About the Project

Transforming Water Board IT Management

The NWSDB Device Record System represents a pivotal digital transformation initiative for the National Water Supply and Drainage Board. We're replacing outdated manual processes with an intelligent, cloud-based platform that brings clarity and control to IT asset management.

Starting with the Bandarawela branch, our mission is to create a comprehensive, accurate inventory of all IT assets across the organization, ensuring accountability, optimizing resource allocation, and enabling data-driven decision making.
